Apple Loans & Leasing

International Information

Educator Loans. Educators, take advantage of a special low-interest-rate loan designed especially for you! The Apple Computer Loan for Educators offers low monthly payments, no downpayment, no pre-payment penalty and a great introductory rate.
Higher Ed Student Loans. The Apple Computer Student Loan has no down payment, no pre-payment penalty, competitive rates, lets you borrow between $1,000 and $7,500, and even allows you to defer principal payments while you're in school.
Consumer Loan. The Apple Computer Consumer Loan allows you to bring home the Apple computer that you want. Monthly payments are low, there is no pre-payment penalty and there are no complicated forms to fill out. Like all Apple Loans, it enables you to purchase the Apple computer, monitor printer and software that you want and need.

Business Lease. Now Apple's business and publishing customers have the financial tools to acquire Apple-based solutions while staying ahead of the technology curve. The Apple Commercial Credit Leasing Program features customized, flexible, easy-to-use lease packages and lease programs--at competitive lease rates.
Education Lease. With the AppleMAX lease program, schools and universities can almost triple their buying power, bringing the benefits of technology to more students. AppleMAX is a leading financial program designed specifically for educational institutions. You can't afford not to learn more.
